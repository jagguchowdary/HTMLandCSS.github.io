Our Tradition

Agricultural Heritage of M.Agraharam, Maddikera (Mandal), Kurnool (Dist) - 518385

Agriculture in our village is not just a means of livelihood; it is a way of life passed down through generations. Our farmers wake up with the sun and work in harmony with nature, respecting the soil and the changing seasons.

Festivals like Pongal, Makar Sankranti, or Baisakhi are celebrated to honor the hard work of farmers and express gratitude for a good harvest.

Even in modern times, we continue to blend traditional methods with new techniques, ensuring that our farming practices remain eco-friendly and efficient.
